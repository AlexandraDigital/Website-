Alexandra Diaz
Digital Illustration
I'm Alexandra Diaz. I'm 22 years old. I'm a student working towards her bachelor's degree in Visual Arts:Electronic Media. I created this website because I need income for a career change in Biomedical Engineering. I'm starting from zero in this field and i need the money to do online learning from a college that is good in that field. I love design but I want to make a difference in people's lives another way. I'm currently a digital artist, as you can see, and i'm hoping this takes off so i can have a job and study remotely.

About Alexandra-Art

Alexandra-Art was born from multiple attempts to create a website that can become a source of income. At first, i was hesitant to use AI however as time passed and this tech became more integrated into our future, i decided to give it a try. My vision with AI as my assistant led to the creation of this website. Now i can sell my digital drawings/designs online for download and do it from the comfort of my home.
